Part One

Privacy Policy

1. Data collection

Darkness collects only the minimal data required to function properly:

  • User IDs — used to identify and manage interactions.
  • Message content — used to process commands and generate responses.
Some collected data is stored permanently.

2. Data usage

Collected data is used exclusively for bot functionality, including:

  • Handling user commands in direct messages or shared servers.
  • Ensuring compliance with Discord's Developer Policy.

Darkness does not analyze data for profiling or advertising purposes.

3. No data sharing

  • Darkness does not sell, share, or distribute any data to third parties.
  • All data is processed and stored internally and is only accessible to the bot's developer team.
  • Data may only be disclosed when required by Discord policy or legal obligation.

4. Retention & requests

  • All data is retained permanently.
  • Users cannot request deletion of their data.
  • Users may request a copy of their data. Requests may take up to two months to process and are delivered by email.
  • There is no opt-out of data collection other than discontinuing use of the bot.
To avoid data collection entirely, do not interact with Darkness and avoid being in any shared server where Darkness is active.

5. Liability

The developers of Darkness are not liable for any form of data leak, breach, or misuse. The bot is used at your own risk.

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of Darkness implies acceptance of any revised terms.

Darkness is a Discord bot that interacts with users through direct messages and within shared servers, providing predefined functionality as described in its documentation. It operates in accordance with Discord's Developer Terms of Service.

3. User conduct

By using the bot, you agree to:

  • Use Darkness responsibly and respectfully.
